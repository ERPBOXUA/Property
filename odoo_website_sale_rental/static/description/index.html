Odoo Website Rental Sale

Now offer products on Rent in your Odoo Website!

Odoo Website Rental Sale allows you to offer products on rent in your Odoo Website. The module lets you manage the rental products and publish them on your online store in Odoo.

Trend of Rental Services among People

Rental services are on trend over the years; especially, among the people who stay far from their families. Rental services are really useful when people want to avail any product for a particular time period. Hence, they need to pay only for the time they have used.

People out for their work or studies prefer to rent products instead of buying as renting is affordable as well as they can only take it for the particular time defined.

From bed to the AC's; from vehicles to the furniture of a house one can rent anything. Basically, renting is an option for those who don't want to buy things for any reason and use the product for a particular time period.

Manage Rental Services in Odoo Website

Now, you can offer rental services to your customers on Odoo website. It means that you can rent out your products and charge for the products on the basis of a particular time period. Odoo Website Rental Sale allows you to manage rent services and offer them hassle-freely to your customers.

You can also manage the contract and agreement of the rental product as per the requirements. The documents need to be managed properly to avoid any kind of hassles.

Your customers can easily visit the frontend and select any rental product to avail and simply pay for the tenure they wish. Hence, you can easily manage rental services and move with the trend to boost the growth of your business.

Manage the Rent Tenure as per Customer's Flexibility

You can set some standard rent tenure along with the price so that your customers can pick anyone as per their need. However, some of the customers may need the product for the time period that is not defined by you.

Offer Flexibility to your customers, now they can not only select from the standard tenure but also customize the rental tenure as they require.

Moreover, you can show the security amount of the rental product to the customers for transparency which builds a relation of trust among customers. It lets the customers choose you again whenever they require to rent a product.

Detailed Features List

Create Rental Products in Odoo Website

  • You can make any product available for rent
  • Once enabled the rental option, you can configure it for renting

Rental Product Management in Odoo Website

  • You can create/manage only their rental products

Manage the Rental Product Agreement

  • You can manage the rental product agreement from Odoo backend
  • You can create multiple agreements and select anyone for a product

Add the Security Amount of the Product

  • You can add the security amount in a rental product
  • The security amount is displayed in the product page of the front-end
  • You can also set Rental Tenure Starting in the product

Set the Rental Tenure Type

  • You can add standard tenure for product rental
  • Standard tenure comprises all the details predefined
  • Custom tenure comprises the UOM of tenure
  • Customer can select UOM and set tenure as they want under custom tenure

Set the Description for Rent

  • You can add the description for the rental product

Rental Products in your Odoo Online Store

  • Rental Products are differentiated with a label in the shop page
  • Moreover, there is a separate section for rental products in the front end

Renew expired rental contract

  • The expired rental contract can also be renewed in Odoo with a click.

Rental details for the customers

  • The customers can see the rental details in the cart page
  • They can also see all their rental orders from My Account section

Different types of tenures to place rental orders

  • You can create and save standard tenures for each product in Odoo.
  • You can then use these tenures while creating rental orders for the customers.
  • You can choose the standard tenure or custom tenure while creating the rental order.
